# Humanoid model research policy v1

Moonfind selects papers deterministically, but MoonClaw performs evidence-bounded interpretation. Every selected paper receives one summary. Claims cite exact paper identifiers and evidence locations. Each idea bridges two or more papers and includes a counter-hypothesis, risks, falsifier, next experiment, and complete humanoid model specification.

The system must distinguish reported evidence from inference and proposal. A named human accepts, revises, or rejects an idea before it can become an experiment or Bookkeeper outcome. Neither Moonfind, Moontown, MoonClaw, nor MoonFlow may self-authorize capability adoption.
